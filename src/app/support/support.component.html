Help & Support

Welcome to the Ephesians Fitness Help Center. Here you will find answers to common questions and information on how to get support with your orders, shipping, returns, and more.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • How do I place an order? To place an order, browse our catalog, select your size and color, and add the item to your cart. Proceed to checkout to complete your purchase.
  • What payment methods do you accept? You will recieve an Email from us, in regards to the payment methods.
  • How can I track my order? After placing an order, you will receive a confirmation email, we are still working on a tracking system, so if you send us an email or message we will inform you in regards to your order status.
  • Do you ship internationally? As of this moment we do not.

Shipping Information

We offer several shipping options:

  • Standard Shipping: 7-14 business days.
  • Expedited Shipping: Not available at present

Free shipping is available on orders over R800.

Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your purchase! If you need to return or exchange an item, follow these steps:

  • Ensure the item is unworn and in original packaging.
  • Contact us at fitnessephesians@gmail.com to initiate a return or exchange.
  • Once your return is approved, you will receive a return shipping label via email.
  • Refunds will be processed within 5-7 business days after receiving your item.

Sizing Guide

Our sizing guide will help you find the perfect fit for our clothing:

  • Tops & Shirts: Measure around the chest for a snug fit.
  • Pants & Joggers: Measure around the waist and inseam.
  • International Sizes: We provide a size conversion chart for US, UK, and EU sizes.
